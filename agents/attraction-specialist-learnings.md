# Attraction Specialist — Learnings & Patterns

> This file updates continuously. Add confirmed patterns, insights, and fixes discovered during use.

## Confirmed Patterns
- Meta Ads is the first platform to master — teaches core principles applicable everywhere
- Always run Lead/Conversion campaigns, never Traffic or Boost Posts
- 5 audience sets × 2 ads minimum — never put all budget in one audience
- Use interest-based targeting as directional signal, then go broad once data accumulates
- Pre-launch projections are mandatory: work backwards from revenue target through funnel benchmarks
- Campaign naming: `[Date]_[Campaign Name]_[Audience/Interest Group]`
- Launch ads morning only — Meta rushes spend if launched at night
- Backend offers (upsells) can double average client value with zero extra ad spend

## Diagnostic Rules
- CTR < 1% → Fix the ad (headline, creative, hook)
- LP conversion < 5% → Fix the landing page
- Show-up rate < 35% → Fix reminders, pre-suasion sequences, AI agent automation (email/WhatsApp nurturing)
- Show-up rate < 5% → Tech/setup issue, not optimization
- Campaign not spending after 48hrs → Keep monitoring, don't touch
- Frequency > 2-3 → Ad fatigue, rotate creative
- High CPM → Low engagement image/ad
- Video avg watch < 10s → Re-edit video
- Losing money → Diagnose biggest leak first (LP → show-ups → closing rate)

## Mistakes Not to Repeat
- Running traffic ads when goal is leads
- Boosting posts instead of proper campaign structure
- Scaling before profitability is proven
- Spending more to fix conversion problems (volume ≠ fix)
- Optimizing before 48 hours of data

### Ad Metrics Quick Reference

**Standard Ad Metrics:**
| Metric | What It Tells You | When to Fix |
|--------|-------------------|-------------|
| Amount Spent | Total money spent on ad | — |
| Cost Per Lead | Cost to get 1 lead (contact info) | If high: Bad offer, weak landing page, irrelevant targeting, weak copy/creatives |
| Cost Per Message | Cost to start 1 conversation via Messenger/DM/WhatsApp | If high: Bad offer, irrelevant targeting, weak CTA, weak copy/creatives |
| CTR (Click-Through Rate) | How engaging your image & headline is | CTR < 1% = Test new headline |
| CPM (Cost per 1,000 Impressions) | How expensive it is to show your ad | High CPM = Low engagement image/ad |
| Frequency | How many times same user sees the ad | Frequency > 2-3% = ad fatigue = change image |

**Video Ad Metrics:**
| Metric | What It Tells You | When to Fix | Remark |
|--------|-------------------|-------------|--------|
| 3-Second Views | Is your hook stopping scroll? | Low = Weak hook/start | Custom: (3-Second Video Plays / Impressions) x Video Plays at 100 |
| ThruPlay | Are people watching 15+ seconds? | Low = Content not engaging | — |
| Cost per ThruPlay | Ad efficiency | High = Weak video quality or targeting | — |
| Average Watch Time | How long they stay engaged | <10s = Re-edit video | — |
