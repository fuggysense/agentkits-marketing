# Conversion Optimizer — Learnings & Patterns

> This file updates continuously. Add confirmed patterns, insights, and fixes discovered during use.

## Confirmed Patterns
- Full funnel metrics chain: Impressions → Clicks → Leads → Show-Ups → Sales → Profit
- Each stage leak amplifies with more spend — fix leaks before scaling
- Small improvements compound: 10% across all stages = 4-6x profit increase
- Show-up rate 30%→35% + closing rate 5%→10% = 2.5x gross profit
- Landing page benchmark: 5-10%+ conversion rate
- Professional stop-loss: set target metrics pre-launch, kill campaign if outside thresholds

## Diagnostic Rules
- LP opt-in < 10% → Landing page issue (test long vs short, headlines, CTAs)
- CTR < 1% + LP opt-in < 10% → Both ad AND landing page need work
- Profitable but low closing → Fix presentation/offer first (highest leverage)
- Break-even → Improve multiple areas concurrently for compound effect
- Losing money → Find single biggest leak, fix it, then move to next

## Mistakes Not to Repeat
- Optimizing all funnel stages equally instead of fixing biggest leak first
- Reporting vanity metrics (impressions, views) instead of CPL, CPA, ROAS
- Ignoring the compounding effect of small incremental improvements

### Ad Metrics Quick Reference

**Standard Ad Metrics:**
| Metric | What It Tells You | When to Fix |
|--------|-------------------|-------------|
| Amount Spent | Total money spent on ad | — |
| Cost Per Lead | Cost to get 1 lead (contact info) | If high: Bad offer, weak landing page, irrelevant targeting, weak copy/creatives |
| Cost Per Message | Cost to start 1 conversation via Messenger/DM/WhatsApp | If high: Bad offer, irrelevant targeting, weak CTA, weak copy/creatives |
| CTR (Click-Through Rate) | How engaging your image & headline is | CTR < 1% = Test new headline |
| CPM (Cost per 1,000 Impressions) | How expensive it is to show your ad | High CPM = Low engagement image/ad |
| Frequency | How many times same user sees the ad | Frequency > 2-3% = ad fatigue = change image |

**Video Ad Metrics:**
| Metric | What It Tells You | When to Fix | Remark |
|--------|-------------------|-------------|--------|
| 3-Second Views | Is your hook stopping scroll? | Low = Weak hook/start | Custom: (3-Second Video Plays / Impressions) x Video Plays at 100 |
| ThruPlay | Are people watching 15+ seconds? | Low = Content not engaging | — |
| Cost per ThruPlay | Ad efficiency | High = Weak video quality or targeting | — |
| Average Watch Time | How long they stay engaged | <10s = Re-edit video | — |
