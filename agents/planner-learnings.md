# Planner — Learnings & Patterns

> This file updates continuously. Add confirmed patterns, insights, and fixes discovered during use.

## Confirmed Patterns
- Pre-launch projections are mandatory before any ad spend — work backwards from revenue target through funnel benchmarks
- Projection framework: Revenue target → needed sales → needed show-ups → needed leads → needed ad spend
- Budget formula: `Total Ad Spend ÷ Days Running = Daily Budget`
- Campaign architecture: 5 audience sets × 2 ads minimum — never all budget in one audience
- First campaign: allow 14 days runtime; subsequent campaigns: 7 days per webinar
- Event cost tiers: Free (office/partner space), ~$30/pax (learning centers, $1k tickets), ~$100/pax (hotel, $3k+ tickets)
- Set target metrics pre-launch as stop-loss — kill campaign if outside thresholds

## Diagnostic Rules
- If projections show negative profit → Fix offer, pricing, or cost structure BEFORE launching
- If model requires unrealistic conversion rates to break even → Wrong category or wrong offer
- Budget too low for 5 ad sets → Reduce ad sets or increase budget to $30-50/ad set/day minimum

## Mistakes Not to Repeat
- Skipping pre-launch projections and "hoping" the numbers work out
- Not calculating total profitability (Revenue − Ad Spend − Venue = Profit) before committing budget
- Planning without benchmark conversion rates — always use: LP 5-10%, CTR >1%, show-up 35%+, close 5-15%

## Reference Data
- Projection example: 10 clients × $3k = $30k revenue. Need 100 show-ups → 250 leads → ~$10k ad spend at $40 CPL
- Webinar cadence: 4-6 webinars over 2 months to fill 10 seats
- Minimum daily spend for meaningful data: $100-$200/day across campaign
