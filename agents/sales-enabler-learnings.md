# Sales Enabler — Learnings & Patterns

> This file updates continuously. Add confirmed patterns, insights, and fixes discovered during use.

## Confirmed Patterns
- Closing rate benchmark: 5-15% (varies by price point)
- Closing rate is the final high-leverage point in the ad funnel — doubling it (5%→10%) halves CPA and doubles profit
- Presentation quality directly drives closing rate — fix presentation before blaming ads or traffic
- Backend offers ($18k-$20k) added to front-end ($3k) can double average client value with zero extra ad spend

## Diagnostic Rules
- Good show-ups but low sales → Fix presentation, offer structure, or closing sequence (not ads)
- Closing rate below 5% on $3k offer → Presentation/offer mismatch with audience expectations
- Profitable campaign but margins thin → Add backend offer to increase LTV, not more ad spend

## Mistakes Not to Repeat
- Blaming ads for low sales when the problem is the presentation or offer
- Not having a backend offer — leaves massive revenue on the table
- Asking agency to "fix ads" when closing rate is the bottleneck

## Reference Data
- Closing rate benchmark: 5-15% (varies by price point and offer)
- Backend conversion rate: 5-10%
- Formula: `Avg Client Value = Front-End Price + (Backend Price × Backend Conversion Rate)`
- Doubling LTV = doubles what you can spend to acquire clients = structural competitive moat
