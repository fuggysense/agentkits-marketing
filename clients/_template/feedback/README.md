# Feedback

Store client quotes, post-mortems, approval notes, and market feedback here.

## Format

- Filename: `YYMMDD-<topic>.md` (e.g., `260312-launch-retro.md`)
- One file per feedback event
- Raw, unedited feedback preferred — distill confirmed patterns into `../learnings.md`

## What belongs here

- Client quotes and reactions (positive and negative)
- Campaign post-mortems
- Approval notes with reasoning
- Market feedback from prospects/customers
- A/B test results and interpretations

## What does NOT belong here

- Campaign assets (go in `campaigns/<slug>/assets/`)
- Reusable creative (go in `../assets/`)
- General marketing knowledge (go in skill/agent learnings)
