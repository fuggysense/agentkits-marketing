# Story Bank

> Mined from discovery interviews. Each story is tagged by SIREN elements, engagement tier, and status.
> Use with the `linkedin-content` skill for post drafting.

## Usage Instructions

1. **Fill this during Phase 1 (Client Discovery Interview)** of the linkedin-content pipeline
2. **Tag every story** with SIREN elements, tier, angle, and hook ideas
3. **Track status** as stories move through the pipeline: Unused → Drafted → Published → Performed well / Underperformed
4. **Never use a Tier 1 story for a Tier 3 post** — Tier 1 stories get standalone posts where the story IS the content

## Engagement Tiers

| Tier | Description | Expected Likes | Use For |
|------|-------------|----------------|---------|
| 1 | High vulnerability/stakes — deeply personal moments | 150+ | Standalone story posts (Pattern 1: Vulnerability Narrative) |
| 2 | Moderate — contrarian takes, relationship stories | 80-150 | Contrarian reframes, gratitude posts (Patterns 2-3) |
| 3 | Tactical/educational — frameworks, insights | 40-80 | Named insights, how-to wrapped in story (Pattern 4) |
