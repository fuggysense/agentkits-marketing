# CRO Learnings

Consolidated from [[page-cro]], [[form-cro]], [[popup-cro]], [[signup-flow-cro]], [[onboarding-cro]], and [[paywall-upgrade-cro]].

## Page CRO

### Confirmed Patterns

- Landing page conversion rate benchmark: 5-10%+ for Meta Ads traffic
- LP opt-in rate < 10% = landing page issue (not ad issue)
- Test long-form vs short-form landing pages when conversion is low
- Different headline/CTA combinations can dramatically shift conversion rates

### Diagnostic Rules

- CTR good (>1%) but leads low -> Landing page is the problem, not the ad
- LP conversion < 5% -> Test: headline, CTA, form length, social proof, page length
- Both CTR < 1% AND LP < 5% -> Fix both, but continue running ads while tweaking LP
