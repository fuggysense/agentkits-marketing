# Discovery Questions — Client Onboarding

Structured question bank for Phase 2 interview. Ask conversationally, not as a form.

---

## ICP Questions

### Demographics
1. What industry or vertical does your ideal client work in?
2. What's their company size? (solo, 1-10, 11-50, 51-200, 200+)
3. Revenue range? (pre-revenue, <$1M, $1-10M, $10-50M, $50M+)
4. Geographic focus? (global, specific countries/regions)
5. Who's the decision maker? (title/role — e.g., CEO, VP Marketing, Head of Product)

### Psychographics
6. What's their #1 pain point right now?
7. What have they already tried to solve it? (and why didn't it work?)
8. What does success look like for them in 6-12 months?
9. What are they afraid of? (biggest fear related to this problem)
10. What do they secretly desire? (aspirational outcome)

### Buying Behavior
11. What's their typical budget for a solution like yours?
12. How long does it take them to make a buying decision?
13. What triggers them to start looking for a solution?
14. What are their top objections before buying?

### Where They Congregate
15. Where do they spend time online? (communities, forums, social platforms)
16. What publications, podcasts, or newsletters do they follow?
17. What events or conferences do they attend?
18. Which social platforms are they most active on?

### Dream Client
19. If you could clone one existing client, who would it be and why?

## Offer Questions

### Core Offer
1. What do you sell? Give me a one-line description.
2. What's the price? (or pricing tiers if multiple)
3. How is it delivered? (done-for-you, done-with-you, DIY, software, physical product)

### Value Proposition
4. What's the #1 benefit your customer gets?
5. Name 3 supporting benefits.
6. What makes you different from alternatives? (not "better" — different)

### Proof Elements
7. Do you have case studies? (even 1 counts)
8. Any testimonials or reviews?
9. Any data points that prove your offer works? (%, time saved, revenue generated)

### Risk Reversal
10. Do you offer a guarantee? (money-back, results guarantee, free trial)
11. How do you reduce the risk for the buyer?

### Urgency / Scarcity
12. Is there any natural urgency? (limited spots, seasonal, price increase coming)
13. Any scarcity levers? (cohort-based, waitlist, limited availability)

## Brand Voice Questions

### Tone
1. If this brand were a person at a party, how would they talk? (formal, casual, witty, authoritative, friendly)
2. Any tone adjustments specific to this project vs your personal voice?

### Terminology
3. Are there terms you always use? (e.g., "clients" not "customers", "invest" not "pay")
4. Any terms to avoid? (competitor names, jargon, words that feel off-brand)

### Messaging Pillars
5. What are your 3-4 core messaging themes? (e.g., "speed", "simplicity", "proven results")

### Examples
6. Share 1-2 sentences that feel perfectly on-brand.
7. Share 1-2 sentences that feel totally off-brand (so we know what to avoid).

---

## Channels Questions

### Acquisition
1. Where do most of your customers come from today?
2. What are your primary marketing channels? (organic search, paid ads, social, referral, cold outreach, partnerships)

### Social
3. Which social platforms do you use? (LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, YouTube)
4. How often do you post? (daily, few times/week, weekly, rarely)

### Paid
5. Do you run paid ads? If so, which platforms? (Meta, Google, LinkedIn, TikTok)
6. What's your monthly ad budget? (even a rough range helps)

### Content
7. Which content types do you produce? (blog, podcast, YouTube, newsletter, other)
8. How often do you publish content?

### Market
9. What country/region is your primary market?
10. What language(s) do you market in?
11. What currency do you price in?
12. What timezone does your team operate in?
