# Identity Frameworks

## Purpose
Steps 3-4 of the offer-building process. These frameworks connect WHO you are to WHY someone should buy from you. Identity is the bridge between raw material (Step 1) and the offer itself.

---

## Framework 1: The House Framework

**Concept:** Your offer is a house. Each part has a job.

```
        🏠 ROOF = Promise
       (What the buyer gets — the outcome)
      ┌──────────────────────────┐
      │    WALLS = Mechanism     │
      │  (How you deliver it —   │
      │   your unique method)    │
      ├──────────────────────────┤
      │  FOUNDATION = Identity   │
      │  (Why YOU — credibility, │
      │   story, proof)          │
      └──────────────────────────┘
        LAND = Market
       (Who it's for — the buyer)
```

### Building the House

**Land (Market):** From Raw Pour Q2 — who is the frustrated buyer?
- Narrow > broad. "SaaS founders doing $1-5M ARR" beats "business owners"
- The tighter the land, the taller the house can be

**Foundation (Identity):** From Raw Pour Q1 + Q7 — why should they trust YOU?
- Personal experience with the problem
- Proprietary process or framework
- Track record / credentials
- Unique access or data

**Walls (Mechanism):** From Raw Pour Q7 + Q8 — what's your unique approach?
- Name your method (e.g., "The 90-Day Revenue Sprint")
- Make it feel proprietary even if the components are common
- The mechanism answers "why is THIS different from everything else I've tried?"

**Roof (Promise):** From Raw Pour Q4 + Q10 — what transformation do you deliver?
- Specific, measurable, time-bound when possible
- "Go from X to Y in Z timeframe" format
- Must be believable given your proof

### Output Format

```
## Offer House

**Land:** [specific market segment]
**Foundation:** [why you — 1-2 sentences]
**Walls:** [your mechanism — named method or unique approach]
**Roof:** [the promise — specific outcome]

One-liner: "I help [land] go from [current state] to [roof] using [walls]"
```

## Framework 2: The 11PM Thought

**Concept:** What is your buyer thinking about at 11PM when they can't sleep? That's your real market — not demographics, not firmographics. The thought that keeps them up.

### The Exercise

Ask: "Close your eyes. It's 11PM. Your ideal customer is lying in bed, staring at the ceiling. What thought keeps looping in their head?"

**Categories of 11PM Thoughts:**

| Category | Example Thought | Offer Angle |
|----------|----------------|-------------|
| **Fear** | "What if this doesn't work and I have to go back to my old job?" | Safety, guarantee, proven method |
| **Frustration** | "I've tried everything and nothing moves the needle" | New mechanism, fresh approach |
| **Ambition** | "I know I could be doing 10x this if I just had the right system" | Unlock, accelerate, shortcut |
| **Shame** | "Everyone else seems to have figured this out except me" | No-judgment, step-by-step, community |
| **Overwhelm** | "There's too much to do and I don't know what matters" | Simplify, prioritize, done-for-you |
| **Impatience** | "I need results NOW, not in 6 months" | Speed, quick wins, fast implementation |

### Connecting to the Offer

The 11PM Thought becomes:
1. **Your headline** — speak the thought back to them
2. **Your positioning** — address the emotional state, not just the logical need
3. **Your guarantee** — directly neutralize the fear behind the thought
4. **Your urgency** — connect to why they can't keep waiting

### Output Format

```
## 11PM Thought Map

**The thought:** "[exact thought in their words]"
**Category:** [fear/frustration/ambition/shame/overwhelm/impatience]
**Headline angle:** [speak the thought back]
**Promise angle:** [what you're promising to resolve]
**Proof needed:** [what evidence neutralizes this specific doubt]
```

---

## Combining Both Frameworks

The House gives you STRUCTURE. The 11PM Thought gives you EMOTION.

Together they produce:
- **Positioning statement:** House one-liner + emotional resonance
- **Identity map:** Who you are × who they are × why this match works
- **Copy ammunition:** Specific phrases, angles, and proof points for every downstream asset

Both outputs feed into the Micro Offer (Step 5) and the full Offer Document (Step 9).
