# Paid Advertising — Learnings & Patterns

> This file updates continuously. Add confirmed patterns, insights, and fixes discovered during use.

## Confirmed Patterns
- Meta charges CPM not CPC — better ads = more clicks per impression = lower effective cost
- Always use Lead/Conversion objective, never Traffic or Engagement
- Launch ads in the morning only — night launches waste budget
- Never pause profitable campaigns — algorithm learning resets
- 5 audience sets x 2 ads minimum per campaign
- Broad targeting outperforms interest targeting once you have data
- Minimum 2 days + $50 spend before making optimization decisions
- Fix biggest funnel leak first, don't spray optimization everywhere
- 10% improvement across all funnel stages = 4-6x profit increase (compounding)
- Only scale when profitable — scaling broken campaigns multiplies losses

## Diagnostic Rules
- CTR < 1% -> Ad issue (headline/creative/hook)
- LP conversion < 5% -> Landing page issue
- Show-up rate < 35% -> Fix reminders, pre-suasion, AI agent automation (email/WhatsApp), webinar timing
- Show-up rate < 5% -> Tech/setup issue, not optimization
- Closing rate below benchmark -> Fix presentation, offer, closing sequence
- CPM too high -> Low engagement image/ad, test new creative
- Frequency > 2-3 -> Ad fatigue, change image
- Campaign not spending -> Keep monitoring, don't touch for 48hrs
- Video avg watch time < 10s -> Re-edit video, weak content
- 3-second views low -> Weak hook/start of video

## Mistakes Not to Repeat
- Running traffic ads instead of lead/conversion ads
- Boosting posts instead of running proper campaigns
- Setting end dates on campaigns (resets algorithm)
- Launching campaigns at night
- Scaling before campaign is profitable
- Spending more money to fix a conversion problem
- Optimizing with less than 48 hours of data
- Hyper-narrow targeting instead of letting Meta's algorithm optimize

### Ad Metrics Quick Reference

**Standard Ad Metrics:**
| Metric | What It Tells You | When to Fix |
|--------|-------------------|-------------|
| Amount Spent | Total money spent on ad | — |
| Cost Per Lead | Cost to get 1 lead (contact info) | If high: Bad offer, weak landing page, irrelevant targeting, weak copy/creatives |
| Cost Per Message | Cost to start 1 conversation via Messenger/DM/WhatsApp | If high: Bad offer, irrelevant targeting, weak CTA, weak copy/creatives |
| CTR (Click-Through Rate) | How engaging your image & headline is | CTR < 1% = Test new headline |
| CPM (Cost per 1,000 Impressions) | How expensive it is to show your ad | High CPM = Low engagement image/ad |
| Frequency | How many times same user sees the ad | Frequency > 2-3% = ad fatigue = change image |

**Video Ad Metrics:**
| Metric | What It Tells You | When to Fix | Remark |
|--------|-------------------|-------------|--------|
| 3-Second Views | Is your hook stopping scroll? | Low = Weak hook/start | Custom: (3-Second Video Plays / Impressions) x Video Plays at 100 |
| ThruPlay | Are people watching 15+ seconds? | Low = Content not engaging | — |
| Cost per ThruPlay | Ad efficiency | High = Weak video quality or targeting | — |
| Average Watch Time | How long they stay engaged | <10s = Re-edit video | — |
